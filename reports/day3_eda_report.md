# Day 3: Exploratory Data Analysis Report

## Objective

Perform exploratory data analysis on mutual fund datasets and identify trends, patterns, and investor behavior.

## Analysis Performed

* NAV trend analysis across 40 schemes
* AUM growth analysis by fund house
* SIP inflow trend analysis
* Category-wise inflow heatmap
* Investor demographic analysis
* Geographic distribution analysis
* Folio growth trend analysis
* NAV return correlation analysis
* Sector allocation analysis
* Additional risk and performance visualizations

## Key Findings

1. SIP inflows showed strong growth from 2022–2025.
2. Industry folio counts nearly doubled during the analysis period.
3. Equity-oriented schemes attracted the majority of investor inflows.
4. AUM is concentrated among a few major fund houses.
5. Investor participation increased significantly in T30 cities.
6. Large-cap funds exhibited relatively stable NAV growth.
7. Sector allocations were concentrated in a small number of sectors.
8. Several funds demonstrated strong risk-adjusted returns.
9. Monthly inflows displayed clear seasonal patterns.
10. Correlation analysis revealed clusters of similar fund behavior.

## Deliverables

* EDA_Analysis.ipynb
* 15+ visualizations
* Exported PNG charts
* Documented insights

## Conclusion

The EDA phase successfully identified investment trends, fund performance patterns, investor demographics, and industry growth characteristics that will support dashboard development and advanced analytics.
